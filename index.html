How to Play

🎮 Objective

Protect your garden house from waves of zombies using powerful plants!

🌱 Plants

  • Sun Flower (🌻) - Generates sun energy every few seconds
  • Pea Shooter (🌱) - Shoots peas at enemies in its row
  • Wall Nut (🥜) - Blocks enemies with high HP
  • Ice Shroom (🍄) - Shoots ice that slows enemies
  • Cherry Bomb (🍒) - Explodes after 2 seconds, damaging all nearby
  • And 18 more to unlock!

👾 Enemies

  • Basic Zombie: Slow walker, easy to kill
  • Cone Head: Has extra armor
  • Speedy: Very fast but low HP
  • Flying: Ignores ground plants - use Cattail!
  • Boss: Massive HP appears every 5 levels

⚡ Tips

  • Start with Sun Flowers for energy income
  • Place shooters behind Wall Nuts
  • Ice Shrooms are great for slowing groups
  • Save Cherry Bombs for emergencies
  • Upgrade plants permanently between levels

🎯 Controls

  • Tap plant card in top bar to select
  • Tap grid cell to place selected plant
  • Tap sun/coins falling to collect
  • P key: Pause game
  • ESC: Deselect plant
